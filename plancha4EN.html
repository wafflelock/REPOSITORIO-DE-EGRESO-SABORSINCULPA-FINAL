Crispy Bacon

Preparation:

  • Place the bacon strips in a cold griddle or skillet over medium heat.
  • Cook the bacon slowly, turning occasionally, until golden and crispy.
  • Reduce heat if necessary to prevent burning.
  • Once cooked, remove the bacon and place it on paper towels to absorb excess fat.
  • Serve hot as a side dish or in sandwiches.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g of bacon strips
  • Paper towels (to absorb excess fat)

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 20 g
  • Carbohydrates: 0 g
  • Fats: 25 g
