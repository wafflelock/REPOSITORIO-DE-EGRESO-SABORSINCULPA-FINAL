Meat Lasagna

Preparation:

  • Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F).
  • In a large skillet, heat oil and add onion and garlic, cooking until golden brown.
  • Add ground meat and cook until well browned.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and herbs to taste. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  • In a deep dish, layer sauce, lasagna sheets, and cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
  • Top with a final layer of sauce and cheese.
  • Bake for 30-35 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  • Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g ground meat (beef or pork)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 800 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 200 g grated mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Herbs to taste (oregano, basil, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Macronutrients per serving (approx.):

  • Calories: 600
  • Protein: 30 g
  • Carbohydrates: 50 g
  • Fat: 25 g
