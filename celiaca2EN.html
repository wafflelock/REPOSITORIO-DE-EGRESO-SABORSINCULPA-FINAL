Gluten-Free Chocolate Cake

Preparation:

  • Prepare the cake:
  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • Grease a 20 cm round cake pan with butter and dust with gluten-free flour.
  • In a large bowl, melt the chocolate and butter in a double boiler or microwave, stirring until smooth.
  • In another bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until frothy and pale.
  • Add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture and mix well.
  • Incorporate the gluten-free flour and salt, mixing until there are no lumps.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing.
  • Serve plain or with your favorite cream or ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • For the cake:
  • 200 g of gluten-free dark chocolate
  • 100 g of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 g of sugar
  • 100 g of gluten-free flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Approximate Macronutrients per Serving:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 5 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fat: 25 g
