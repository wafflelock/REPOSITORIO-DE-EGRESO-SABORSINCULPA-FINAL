Grilled Zucchini

Preparation:

  • Wash and slice the zucchini into 1 cm thick rounds.
  • In a bowl, mix olive oil, salt, pepper, and herbs to taste.
  • Dip the zucchini slices into the mixture to soak up the flavors.
  • Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and add the zucchini slices.
  • Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden and tender.
  • Serve hot, either with a sauce or as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Herbs (oregano, thyme) to taste

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 80
  • Protein: 2 g
  • Carbohydrates: 8 g
  • Fats: 5 g
