Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Preparation:

  • Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  • Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  • While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  • Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until golden brown.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Drain the pasta and mix with the tomato sauce.
  • Serve hot, sprinkling grated cheese on top if desired.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 800 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves to taste
  • Grated cheese to taste (optional)

Macronutrients per serving (approx.):

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 12 g
  • Carbohydrates: 60 g
  • Fat: 10 g
