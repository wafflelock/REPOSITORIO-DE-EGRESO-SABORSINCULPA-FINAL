Baked Fish with Vegetables

Preparation:

  • Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  • In a baking dish, place the fish fillets.
  • Drizzle olive oil over the fish and season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  • Add sliced bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes around the fish.
  • Sprinkle garlic powder and dried herbs over the vegetables.
  • Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  • Serve the baked fish with the roasted vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 4 fish fillets (e.g., cod, tilapia, or salmon)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano or thyme

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30 g
  • Carbohydrates: 15 g
  • Fat: 15 g
