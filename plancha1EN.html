Lemon Herb Salmon with Vegetables

Preparation:

  • In a bowl, mix lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Place the salmon in a dish and pour the mixture over it. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, prepare the vegetables. You can use asparagus, carrots, and bell peppers cut into strips.
  • Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and add a little oil.
  • Cook the salmon for 5-6 minutes on each side until golden and cooked to your liking.
  • In the same grill, add the vegetables and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Serve the salmon hot alongside the vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetables of your choice (asparagus, carrots, peppers)

Macronutrients per serving (approx.):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30 g
  • Carbohydrates: 10 g
  • Fat: 20 g
