Gluten-Free Brownies

Preparation:

  • Brownie Mixture:
  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a 20x20 cm square baking pan.
  • In a large bowl, melt the butter and chocolate over low heat or in the microwave.
  • Add the sugar and mix well.
  • Incorporate the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Add the vanilla extract and mix until smooth.
  • Sift the gluten-free flour, cocoa powder, and salt, then fold into the mixture until no lumps remain.
  • Pour the mixture into the greased pan and smooth the surface.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  • Let cool in the pan before cutting into portions.

Ingredients:

  • For the brownie mixture:
  • 150 g of gluten-free dark chocolate
  • 100 g of butter
  • 200 g of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 100 g of gluten-free flour
  • 30 g of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 4 g
  • Carbohydrates: 35 g
  • Fat: 12 g
