Grilled Chicken Breast with Chimichurri Sauce

Preparation:

  • In a bowl, mix the chopped parsley, garlic, vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the chimichurri sauce.
  • Marinate the chicken breast in the sauce for at least 30 minutes.
  • Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and add a little oil.
  • Cook the chicken breast for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Serve the grilled chicken breast hot with chimichurri sauce on top.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 35 g
  • Carbohydrates: 5 g
  • Fats: 28 g
