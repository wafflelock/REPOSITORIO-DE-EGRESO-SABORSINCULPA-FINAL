Vegan Tacos

Preparation:

  • In a pan, heat a little oil and add onion and bell pepper. Sauté until tender.
  • Add the crumbled tofu and season with spices to taste (cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper). Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Warm the tortillas in another pan or microwave.
  • Fill each tortilla with the tofu and vegetable mixture, and add your favorite toppings like avocado, salsa, or cilantro.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your vegan tacos.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g tofu
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Spices to taste (cumin, paprika, salt, pepper)
  • Avocado (optional)
  • Salsa to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 15 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fats: 10 g
