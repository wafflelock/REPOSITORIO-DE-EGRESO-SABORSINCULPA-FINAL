Curry Chicken

Preparation:

  • Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
  • Sauté the chicken until golden brown on all sides.
  • Add the onion and garlic, and cook until translucent.
  • Stir in the curry, turmeric, and cumin, and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add the crushed tomatoes and coconut milk, and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and cook for 20 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve the curry chicken with white rice.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
  • White rice for serving

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 500
  • Protein: 35 g
  • Carbohydrates: 45 g
  • Fat: 20 g
