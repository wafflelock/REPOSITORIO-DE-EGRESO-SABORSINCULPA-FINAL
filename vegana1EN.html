Lentil Burger

Preparation:

  • Cook the lentils in salted water until tender, about 30 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  • In a bowl, mix the cooked lentils with chopped onion, minced garlic, grated carrot, and spices to taste.
  • Add breadcrumbs until the mixture is manageable.
  • Form burgers with the mixture and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  • Heat a skillet with a little oil and cook the burgers for 5-7 minutes on each side until golden.
  • Serve in buns with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Spices to taste (pepper, cumin, etc.)
  • Oil for frying
  • Buns and toppings of choice for serving

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 12 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fats: 8 g
