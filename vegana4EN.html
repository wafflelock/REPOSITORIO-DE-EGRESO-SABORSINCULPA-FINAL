Quinoa Salad

Preparation:

  • Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool.
  • In a large bowl, mix the cooked quinoa with cucumber, tomato, red onion, and bell peppers.
  • Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste.
  • Mix all the ingredients well and serve cold.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 8 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fats: 9 g
