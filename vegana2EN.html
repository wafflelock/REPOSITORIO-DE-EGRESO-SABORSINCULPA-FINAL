Pumpkin Cream

Preparation:

  • Peel and cut the pumpkin into small pieces.
  • In a pot, heat a little oil and sauté the onion and garlic until golden.
  • Add the pumpkin and vegetable broth. Cook over medium heat for about 20-25 minutes, or until the pumpkin is tender.
  • Remove from heat and blend the mixture until smooth.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve hot, garnished with pumpkin seeds or a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pumpkin
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 liter vegetable broth
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pumpkin seeds (optional, for garnish)

Macronutrients per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 3 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fats: 5 g
